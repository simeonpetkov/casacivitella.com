The estate consists of a classical tuscan stone house (480 sqm habitable space on 2 floors + attic), a large greenhouse, a building for agricultural machinery, a 12 x 5 pool and a chicken coop for 20 chickens.


The house was renovated in 2000 and refurbished. The owner, a retired physician and music lover, modified the house to his taste and lovingly restored it without affecting it's classical look. For reasons of age, he is no longer able to take care of the property and offers it for sale.


The first floor consists of a large kitchen, a living room with a fireplace (80 sqm), a dining room, an interspace with a bread-oven, a storage room, a large bathroom with a window, a laundry room and a control-room for the automatic central heating.


The second floor consists of a living room with fireplace, a walk-in closet with ironing area, and four bedrooms. The master-bedroom has a spacious bathroom equipped with a bath-tub and a shower, two of the bedrooms have bathrooms with showers and one bedroom is without bathroom. All bathrooms have windows.


The attic is a former library.


The two terraces (east and west) offer beautiful views: the southeast terrace to Civitella and the west terrace in direction tuscan hills.


The pool (12x5) offers beautiful views of the surrounding hills. It is equipped with a machine room, a changing room and showers. The water supply is completely from the private well.


The land (13 hectares, about half of them forest) is agriculturally cultivated (500 grapevines, 220 olive trees, 150 old and 70 young fruit trees and a vegetable garden). A distillation facility for grappa is on site. The olive trees allow for a production of around 200kg olive oil annually. There is a lot of flowering, decorative trees, pines and mediterranean shrubs surrounding the house.


The greenhouse is connected to the central heating.


Central automatic heating covers the main house and the greenhouse.


Digital connections: telephone land-line, satellite TV and ADSL internet.


Location: the house is located in the tuscan hills, 2 km outside of Civitella. Civitella's ancient castle offers year round thetrical performances and is one of Tuscany's lively villages. The house itself is reached by a 400m long private road, coming off the Sienna-Arrzzo state-road. In the surrounding are one can find many of italy's cultural hotstpots and numerous theraml baths.
